## 🚀 About Me 

I'm a Data Analyst and aspiring Data Scientist passionate about transforming raw data into meaningful insights and data-driven solutions. I enjoy exploring datasets, uncovering patterns, and building analytical tools that help organizations make informed decisions.

My interests span Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, Data Science, and Machine Learning. I work with technologies such as Python, SQL, Power BI, and Excel to clean data, create compelling visualizations, develop predictive models, and solve real-world business problems.

I am continuously learning and building projects that strengthen my skills in analytics, machine learning, and data storytelling. Through this GitHub portfolio, I share my journey, projects, and experiments as I grow into a well-rounded data professional.

When I'm not working with data, you'll find me reading business and analytics case studies, hiking new trails, on the basketball court, travelling to explore different places and cultures, or mentoring others who are beginning their own learning journeys. I enjoy continuous growth, meaningful conversations, and discovering new perspectives. I love the "aha!" moment when data reveals something new and useful, transforming complex information into insights that drive better decisions and real-world impact.
